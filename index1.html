Student Propulsion & Aerodynamics Collective

A student-run aerospace research group building and testing efficient airframes, guidance, and recovery systems for competitions like the American Rocketry Challenge.

Explore Programs Join the Team

Mission

We give motivated students a professional-grade sandbox to learn aerodynamics, propulsion, structures, control, and test methodology by building real hardware and flying it safely under NAR codes. Our ethos: design → simulate → test → iterate → document.

Education First

Workflows mirror real labs: OpenRocket/RASAero sims, mass‑trim logs, parachute sizing, failure notes, and flight readiness checklists.

Repeatable Engineering

The goal isn’t one perfect flight — it’s consistent performance under changing conditions.

Programs & Focus

TARC 2025 Team

Build a compliant egg‑payload rocket tuned for the target altitude/time window. Emphasis on drag management, mass trim, and parachute descent rates.
